The Process

Up and running in minutes

Two simple flows — one for founders, one for creators. No gatekeeping, no long forms.

02

Discover & evaluate

Founders browse the creator directory with smart filters — niche, audience size, engagement. Creators get passive visibility to hundreds of serious startup clients every week.

03

Collaborate on WhatsApp

Click Collaborate — WhatsApp opens instantly with a pre-filled message. No email threads. No waiting. Just a real conversation with the right person.

FAQ

Your questions, answered

How does the WhatsApp collaboration work?
When a founder clicks Collaborate on a creator profile, PostHire opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Free plan: WhatsApp revealed after the creator accepts the request. Starter: shown when request is sent (20/month). Pro: WhatsApp always visible, unlimited requests.
Can creators sign up for free?
Yes! Creators can sign up and set up their full profile completely free. You'll be listed in the creator directory and receive collaboration requests from founders. Pro creators get featured placement and top search visibility.
What niches are supported?
PostHire supports 10 niches: AI, Finance, Startup, Tech, Lifestyle, Business, Marketing, SaaS, Education, and Health. Creators can select multiple niches and founders can filter by any combination of these.
Is PostHire only for Indian creators and startups?
PostHire is built for India first — pricing is in INR and the focus is on the Indian startup ecosystem. However, creators and startups from anywhere can join and collaborate on the platform.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes! You can change your plan anytime from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. There are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees.
What's coming in Phase 2?
Phase 2 will include AI-powered creator matching, in-platform messaging, subscription payments, creator rating system, and a full analytics dashboard. Phase 3 will add collaboration contracts, escrow payments, and campaign tracking.
